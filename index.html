About Us

Why TechFore Group

Based in New Jersey, TechFore Group, LLC has been building extraordinary projects since 1996. We have earned a reputation as one of the premier historical contractors in the country. Today, we boast of a 100% on-time completion record, and a proven track record of quality workmanship, integrity and great value.

The principal of TechFore Group, LLC, Mr. Ganga Reddy, previously operated under the name Hilt Construction, Inc. The company was rebranded as TechFore Group, LLC when one of the partners at Hilt Construction retired.

In 1999, TechFore Group, LLC led the restoration of the Lambert Castle in Paterson, NJ. A historical landmark built in 1891 and recorded in the National Register of Historic Places, the Lambert Castle restoration required painstaking attention to detail and quality workmanship. Our successful restoration of the Lambert Castle earned us a reputation as one of the premier specialists in historical restoration in the country.

Today, TechFore Group, LLC continues to deliver projects to institutional, industrial and corporate clients with honesty, integrity and cost-effectiveness, 100% on time.
